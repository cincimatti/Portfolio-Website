Introduction

Matt Carter

Principal Specialist Solutions Architect

Matt Carter serves as a Principal Solutions Architect with Amazon Web Services specilizing in media solutions. With over 20 years of industry experience, a patent holder in video processing, and contributor to the Motion Imagery Standards Board, he has become a well-recognized Subject Matter Expert in video technologies for both commercial and public sector applications. Matt obtained his degree in Applied Science from Miami University and is a veteran of the United States Army Signal Corps.
